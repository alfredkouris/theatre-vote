Chef's Note

Creative Team

Alfred Kouris

Writer, Director & Performer

Alfred Kouris is a Sydney-based theatre maker, writer, and performer. His work explores the intersection of live performance and everyday ritual, creating intimate experiences that blur the boundaries between audience and participant.

Harry Stacey

Writer, Director & Performer

Harry Stacey is a theatre maker and performer known for his innovative approach to live performance. His practice embraces risk, spontaneity, and the unique magic that can only happen in the shared space between performer and audience.

Talia Benater

Producer

Talia Benater is a Sydney-based producer and creative collaborator passionate about supporting bold, experimental theatre. She brings vision and dedication to every project, ensuring artists can take risks and audiences can discover new experiences.

Jasmine Power

Stage Manager

Jasmine Power is a skilled stage manager with a keen eye for detail and a calm presence in the chaos of live performance. Her technical expertise and collaborative spirit ensure smooth operations both on and off stage.

Thomas Shepherd

Lighting & Sound Design

Thomas Shepherd is a lighting and sound designer whose work creates atmospheric and immersive environments. His thoughtful design choices enhance the storytelling and heighten the audience's sensory experience.
